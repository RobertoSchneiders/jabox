Integration with Nexus

The URL should point to your Nexus instance (e.g. http://myserver/nexus/ )

Provide the credentials that Jabox will use to setup release process (a maven settings.xml under $HOME/.jabox/.m2/settings.xml)

When generating a new project, Jabox will configure project distribution management (releases & snapshots) to upload binaries to this repository.

Continuous Integration will be configured by Jabox to use the specific settings.xml during build/release processes.
